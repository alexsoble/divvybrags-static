Go ahead.
Get your stats.

Liberate your data.

What you'll need:

  • A Divvy membership (get one here).
  • The Chrome browser (get it here).

How to use DivvyBrags:

  1. Get the DivvyBrags extension from the Chrome store.
  2. Go to divvybikes.com, log in.
  3. Go to your Trips page.
    You'll see a DivvyBrags sidebar now.
  4. Set your data free!
  5. Get DivvyBrags from the Chrome store.
  6. Go to divvybikes.com, log in.
  7. Go to your Trips page. You'll see a Divvybrags sidebar with a few options.


Enjoy!
